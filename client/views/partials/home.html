ABOUT



Accuracy

School class list and all information for a sale is conveyed to Amulek Consulting for easy loading into computer. All information is kept confidential and secure. All calculations are performed by the computer. Discrepancies are easily identified. All prize lists are automatically generated by the computer.


Information

Because the computer has the names of all of the students in the school and the number of orders they have sold, all prize winners lists are automatically generated. No reports and calculations are manually generated. Special promotions winners, such as “Wear It To Win” or “Peak Performers Club”, are automatically generated and printed out. Estimating the number of prizes for the next year can easily be calculated.


Satisfaction

MSRS will differentiate you from the competition by providing a higher level of service to your schools. The MSRS reports have a professional look that will send the message to the school that you want them to have greater success in their fund raising efforts.


Sales

The MSRS reports will provide the schools with more information for them to heighten the interest the students have in making more sales. The more involved the teachers are in the sales the greater the sales will be. The MSRS reports provide information to create a more competitive environment between the teachers, which will equate to higher sales.
